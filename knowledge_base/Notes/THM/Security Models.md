# Zero Trust vs. Trust but Verify
Trust but verify: always verify even when we trust an entity and its behavior
Zero trust: trust is a vulnerability
- Microsegmentation - segment the network as small as possible
- Goes against business objectives
# Vulnerability, Thread, and Risk
Vulnerability: means susceptible to attack or damage
Threat: potential danger associated with this vulnerability
Risk: likelihood of a threat actor exploiting the vulnerability and the subsequent impact on business
